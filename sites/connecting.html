Connecting bot to own server

  1. Create your server in Discord as indicated in the instructions:
  2. Open website Discord Developer Portal
  3. Select New Application
  4. Name your swage bot
  5. Go to your bot
  6. Copy your TOKEN which we will use in the future (under no circumstances can it be transferred to third parties)
  7. Mark all the markers as in this screenshots:
  8. Go to OAuth2 and follow the instructions:
  9. Copy link and paste into search bar
  10. Done, your bot is already on your server
